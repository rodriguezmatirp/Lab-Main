Since 1933, The Bookworm Bookshop has offered rare and hard -to-find titles for the discerning reader, Unlike the biiger bookstore chains, the Bookworm offers:

  • Friendly, knowledgeable, and courteous help
  • Free coffee and juice for our customers
  • A well lit reading room so you can "try before you buy"
  • Four friendly cats: Esmerelda, Catherine, Dulcinea and Beatrice

Oue hours are 10 am to 9pm weekdays, noon to 7 on weekends.
